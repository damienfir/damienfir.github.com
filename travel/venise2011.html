La Biennale di Venezia

The renowned contemporary art event took place while we were in Venice. The whole city became an exhibition, with art shows scattered all over the place where artists form the whole world showed their work.

We went to the Arsenale and the Giardini for the exhibition called Illuminazioni where countries could exhibit a few artist's work.
